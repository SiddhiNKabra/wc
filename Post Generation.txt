Post Generation

AI social media post generators are trained on a massive dataset of content from social media platforms. 
This dataset includes examples of posts, captions, hashtags, images, videos, and other relevant content types.

Generative AI
Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can generate original text, images, or other types of content. 
These systems are trained on large amounts of data, learning patterns and structures that enable them to create human-like content.

3. Training the model
